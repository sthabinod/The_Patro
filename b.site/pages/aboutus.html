About Us

We at thepatro, can learn and share different cultures, traditions and arts of Nepal. Thepatro offers knowledge about various religions, cultures from all over Nepal. Basically, the word patro refers to a book that has the information about dates, tithi and different special occasions of Nepali culture. Thepatro is the collection of different information about our culture, traditions, art and ethinic groups of Nepal. Different cultures of our country Nepal are on the verge of extinction. With this website, we can learn about different cultures and traditions of castes, ethnic groups and religions.

Our Mission

To preserve our cultures and traditions. To be aware about the vast cultures of Nepal. To preserve the festivals and special occasion that are on the verge of extinction. To know about the different festivals, occasions that are celebrated around our country. To preserve the ethnicity and the ethnic groups of Nepal. To spread our customs, traditions and cultures all around the globe.


Our Vision

Protection and promotion of national cultures, traditions and ethnicity of Nepal within the Nepal as well as spreading our customs all around the globe by providing information about our cultures and traditions.
