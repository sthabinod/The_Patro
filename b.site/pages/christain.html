Christainity

Christinity is the the religion followed all over the world and it is an Abhramic monotheistic religion based on the teachings of Jesus Nazareth. It is believed that Jesus is the Christ, the son of the God and saviour of the people. The beginning of the christinity was on the 1st century of the Roman provience of Judea. Chiristinity was evolved in the western part of the world and it is culturally diversing to the south and north. Likewise, Nepal is country where most of the people are getting the teachings of Christinity. According to the 2011 census, the fifth most practised religion in nepal is Christinity with 1.4% of the population. The first christain mission to Nepal was established in 1715 by Catholic Capuchin friars who worked in Kathmandu Valley.

Orgination and History

Christinity was originated from the Jusus in the 1st Century Roman province of Judea. According to the Gospals, Jesus Christ was the teacher and healer. His followers believed that he was raised from the death. The first and early follower was apocalyptic Jewish Christians. Afterwards, the christinity was divided into the two parts as Judaism and Jewish Christinity. Jesus of Nazareth. From the teachings of Jesus and the acts of his followers after his death, the Christian faith spread across the world.

God and Followers

Gods in christinity is the eternal. In christinity the name of the god has deeper meaning. Christain followers believes that name of God cannot be a human invention. According to the Church Father, the name of the god is seen as representation of the entire system of 'devine truth'. So, Christain believe that there is only one God, whom they call Father as Jesus Christ taught them. Jesus Christ recognises himself as the son of the God to save mankind from the death and sins. Christians believe in the Trinity - that means, in God as Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

Festivals and Celebrations

The most important and the most celebrated day of the christain people is 'Christmas' . Christmas is celebrated all over the world. It is celebrated on 25th of December of every year. It is celebrated as the birth of the Jesus Christ, son of the God. In nepal christmas is celebrated as public rather by the christain peoples only. On that day, Santa ( Father Christmas) who brings gifts to the homes of well-behaved children on the night of Christmas Eve (24 December) and the early morning hours of Christmas Day (25 December). Easter is also one of the most important festival in christinity. It is the day of celebtation that Jesus Christ was rise from the dead. Good Friday is the day before Easter or Pascha. Good Friday is the holiday and it observe by some of the christains.

Holy Books

The name of the holy of the christinity is 'Holy Bible'. The Bible Begins with the Pentateauch. The five books of the Christain Old Testament and the all the Jewish Torah. Understanding the bible requires the understanding the Pentateuch. Pentateauch is originated from the greek words and refers the five scrolls and also terms as the first five books of the Christain Bible. The five books of the Pentateauch are Genesis which means creation, the second one is Exodus which means departure , the third one is Leviticus which means concerning the Levites, fourth one is Numbers and last one is Deuteronomy.
